$$ V_C(r) = \frac{Z_1Z_2e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r} \approx \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r} $$

waves3. RF-Driven Catalytic Enhancement

3.1 Field-Induced Barrier Lowering

An oscillating electric field $E_0 \cos(\omega t)$ induces dynamic Stark shifts:

\[ V_C'(r,t) = V_C(r) - \alpha E_0^2 \cos^2(\omega t) $$
  • $\alpha$: molecular polarizability along bond axis

Time-averaged barrier reduction:

$$ \Delta V \approx \frac{\alpha E_0^2}{2} $$

3.2 Resonant Coupling

Catalyst vibrational mode frequency $\omega_0$ tuned near RF frequency to maximize energy transfer:

$$ H_{int} = -\mu_m E_0 \cos(\omega t)\cos(\omega_0 t) $$

Generates anharmonic mixing and transient localization of energy in the reaction coordinate.
